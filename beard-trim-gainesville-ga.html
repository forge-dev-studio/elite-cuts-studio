Beard Trims & Grooming done right

Great beard work is about symmetry and clean edges — a defined cheek line, a natural taper down the neck, and length that suits your face. At Elite Cuts Studio we shape the beard to your jaw and finish the lineup with a straight razor for that crisp, defined edge.

Add a beard trim to any cut or book it on its own. Either way you’re walking out with a beard that looks groomed, not just grown.

Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

How much is a beard trim in Gainesville?

A simple beard trim is $10, and a premium beard trim (full shape, razor lineup, hot-towel finish) is $20 at Elite Cuts Studio.

Can I add a beard trim to my haircut?

Yes — add it on when you book or ask your barber in the chair. It’s the most popular combo we do.

Do you do straight-razor lineups?

We do. Every premium beard trim finishes with a straight-razor lineup for a defined, crisp edge.

How often should I get my beard trimmed?

Every 2–4 weeks keeps the shape clean. If you’re growing it out, we tidy the edges while letting the length come in.
